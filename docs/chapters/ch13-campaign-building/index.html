Chapter 13: Campaign Building

Create living galactic settings that evolve with character advancement, using the Universal Tag System to maintain consistency across cosmic scales.

13.1 Galactic Setting Design

Your galaxy should feel alive and reactive to PC actions. Use tags to define civilizations, conflicts, and cosmic phenomena.

Civilization Tags

The Terran Federation

Tags: [Democratic], [Expansionist], [Technology-Focused], [Diverse]

Human space democracy with advanced technology and species diversity.

Draconic Empire

Tags: [Ancient], [Honorable], [Warrior-Culture], [Mysterious]

Dragon-descended species with rigid honor codes and hidden knowledge.

13.2 Campaign Themes

Exploration Campaigns

Tags: [Discovery], [Unknown], [Wonder], [Dangerous]

Focus on finding new worlds, species, and phenomena.

War Campaigns

Tags: [Conflict], [Military], [Heroic], [Sacrifice]

Large-scale conflicts between civilizations.

Trade Campaigns

Tags: [Commercial], [Diplomatic], [Competitive], [Profitable]

Economic adventures and merchant politics.

Mystery Campaigns

Tags: [Ancient], [Hidden], [Conspiracy], [Revelation]

Uncover galactic secrets and cosmic truths.

13.3 Campaign Evolution

Campaigns should grow in scope as characters advance:

Tier 1: Local Heroes (Ranks 1-3)

  • Single planet or station adventures
  • Personal stakes and relationships
  • Learning the galaxy's rules

Tier 2: System Operatives (Ranks 4-6)

  • Multi-planet storylines
  • Political intrigue and faction conflicts
  • Building reputation and resources

Tier 3: Galactic Legends (Ranks 7-9)

  • Sector-wide consequences
  • Interactions with galactic powers
  • Shaping the future of civilizations

Tier 4: Cosmic Forces (Rank 10)

  • Universe-threatening events
  • Reality-altering consequences
  • Transcendent character evolution

13.4 Living Galaxy Principles

Dynamic Setting Elements

  • Faction Reactions: Organizations respond to PC actions with appropriate tags
  • Reputation Systems: Character tags influence how NPCs react
  • Evolving Conflicts: Galactic situations change based on PC involvement
  • Tag Inheritance: Successful adventures add tags to locations and organizations
  • Scale Progression: Threats grow to match character capabilities
