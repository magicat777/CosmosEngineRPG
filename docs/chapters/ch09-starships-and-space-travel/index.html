Damage & Consequences

Starships use condition tracks similar to vehicles:

  • Light Damage: -1 to specific systems
  • Moderate Damage: -2 to affected systems, crew injuries
  • Critical Damage: Major system failure, life support threats
  • Destroyed: Ship lost, crew survival depends on escape systems

  • Maintenance: 0.1-1% of ship value/month
  • Fuel: Distance × ship class multiplier
  • Docking: Class-based daily fees
  • Crew: Skill level × number × hazard pay
