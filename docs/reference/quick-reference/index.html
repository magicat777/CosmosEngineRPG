üé≤ Core Resolution Mechanics

Standard Method

2d10 + Attribute Modifier + Equipment + Situation + Tag Dice

  • Attribute Modifier: -2 to +7
  • Skill Tags: +1 to +7 dice per skill
  • Equipment: 0-5 (Tech Era)
  • Situation: -3 to +3
  • Flat Modifier Caps: +8 max, -6 min

Tag Synergy Method

Roll extra d10s, keep best 2

  • +1d10 per matching tag
  • Maximum: 7d10, keep best 2
  • Skill tags gained through advancement ranks
  • Replaces modifier caps when used

‚ö° Status Effects

Combat Conditions

  • Stunned: Lose next action, -2 to defense
  • Wounded: -1 die to all physical actions
  • Exhausted: -1 die to all actions
  • Dying: Incapacitated, needs immediate aid
  • Pinned: Cannot move, -2 to attacks
  • Burning: 1d6 damage per round

Mental Conditions

  • Panicked: Must flee or take -2 dice
  • Confused: Random target selection
  • Charmed: Cannot harm charmer
  • Terrified: Cannot approach source
  • Berserk: +2 damage, -2 defense
  • Focused: +1 die to chosen action

üîß Common Modifiers

Advantages (+)

  • Superior equipment: +1 to +3
  • Taking time: +1 to +2
  • Assistance: +1 per helper
  • Ideal conditions: +1 to +2
  • Height advantage: +1

Disadvantages (-)

  • Poor equipment: -1 to -3
  • Rushed action: -1 to -2
  • Distracted: -1 to -2
  • Bad conditions: -1 to -3
  • Multiple targets: -1 each

Combat Specific

  • Aim action: +1 next attack
  • Cover: +1 to +3 defense
  • Flanking: +1 attack
  • Prone: +2 vs ranged
  • Blind: -3 to actions

‚ö° Actions in Combat

Standard Actions

  • Attack: Roll to hit, deal damage
  • Full Defense: +3 to defense, no attack
  • Move: Up to Speed in meters
  • Aim: +1 to next attack
  • Reload: Ready weapon
  • Use Skill: Non-combat ability

Free Actions

  • Speak: Short communication
  • Drop Item: Release held object
  • Simple Movement: Step, turn
  • Activate: Toggle simple device
  • Observe: Quick perception

üí• Critical Results

Critical Success (Natural 20)

  • Automatic success regardless of TN
  • Double damage on attacks
  • Exceptional result on skill checks
  • May gain temporary tag or advantage
  • Inspire allies (+1 die next action)

Critical Failure (Natural 2)

  • Automatic failure regardless of modifiers
  • Possible equipment malfunction
  • May hit ally or self
  • Lose next action or take penalty
  • Create disadvantage for team
