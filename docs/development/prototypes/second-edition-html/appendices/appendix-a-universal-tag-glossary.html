A

[Accurate] ‚Äî Weapon Tag

Description: Enhanced targeting capability through superior design, advanced sights, or built-in targeting systems.

Mechanical Benefit: +1 to +3 dice when making precision shots or when accuracy is paramount.

Typical Applications: Sniper rifles, hunting weapons, surgical instruments, measurement tools.

Story Hooks: Reputation for never missing may attract attention from those who need problems eliminated quietly.

[Advanced] ‚Äî Technology Tag

Description: Cutting-edge technology that exceeds current standard capabilities through innovative design or superior materials.

Mechanical Benefit: +2 to +4 dice when used by characters with compatible technical tags or in optimal conditions.

Typical Applications: Prototype equipment, military-grade systems, experimental technology.

Story Hooks: May be sought after by competitors, require specialized maintenance, or have unknown side effects.

[Aggressive] ‚Äî Character Tag

Description: Favors direct, forceful approaches to problems. Takes initiative and presses advantages without hesitation.

Mechanical Benefit: +1 to +3 dice when taking the initiative, making first strikes, or applying pressure.

Typical Applications: Combat situations, negotiations where intimidation is effective, leadership roles.

Story Hooks: May escalate situations unnecessarily, make enemies through forceful approach, or inspire others to follow boldly.

[Agile] ‚Äî Physical Tag

Description: Exceptional speed, flexibility, and maneuverability allowing fluid movement through complex environments.

Mechanical Benefit: +1 to +3 dice for dodge actions, parkour, acrobatics, and movement-based tasks.

Typical Applications: Escape attempts, navigating difficult terrain, combat positioning, performance.

Story Hooks: Reputation for impossible escapes, recruited for infiltration missions, or challenged to prove abilities.

[AI] ‚Äî Technology Tag

Description: Artificial intelligence integration providing autonomous decision-making and adaptive responses.

Mechanical Benefit: +2 to +4 dice for complex calculations, pattern recognition, and multitasking operations.

Typical Applications: Ship systems, combat analysis, data processing, automated operations.

Story Hooks: AI may develop unexpected personality, conflict with other systems, or become target for hackers.

[Analytical] ‚Äî Mental Tag

Mechanical Benefit: +1 to +3 dice when researching, investigating, or solving puzzles requiring logical thinking.

Typical Applications: Scientific research, detective work, tactical planning, technical troubleshooting.

Story Hooks: May be consulted for complex problems, overthink simple situations, or uncover secrets others missed.

[Armored] ‚Äî Equipment Tag

[Artistic] ‚Äî Character Tag

[Athletic] ‚Äî Physical Tag

Story Hooks: Past athletic achievements, competition rivalries, or recruitment for physical missions.

[Augmented] ‚Äî Character Tag
