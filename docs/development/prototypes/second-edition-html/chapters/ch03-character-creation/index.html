Quick Start Path (New Players)

üöÄ Create Your First Character in 15 Minutes

  1. Pick a Concept: Smuggler, Soldier, Scientist, or Pilot
  2. Choose Human: No modifiers, most flexible
  3. Use Standard Array: 5, 4, 4, 3, 3, 2 for attributes
  4. Take a Skill Package: Explorer, Specialist, Soldier, or Socialite
  5. Select Tags: One per skill rank (automatic with package)
  6. Calculate Values: Use the formulas below
  7. Grab Starting Gear: Pick an equipment package
  8. Name & Motivate: Who are you and what drives you?
  9. Connect: How do you know the other characters?

üìä Two Separate AP Pools

You get TWO separate pools of 10 AP each:

  • 10 AP for Attributes - Spend on your six PRIMAC scores
  • 10 AP for Skills - Spend on skill ranks and specializations

These pools NEVER mix. You cannot transfer points between them.

The Nine Steps

Step 1: Concept (1 minute)

Answer these questions:

  • What's your profession or role?
  • Where are you from?
  • What's your greatest strength?
  • What's your biggest flaw?
Quick Background Generator (Optional)

Roll 2d10 or choose: First die = Career, Second die = Defining Event

1. Military 2. Criminal 3. Academic 4. Corporate 5. Spacer
6. Medical 7. Explorer 8. Entertainer 9. Religious 10. Freelance

Step 2: Choose Species

Select from 10 species. See complete comparison table below or choose Human for simplicity.

Full species descriptions available in Chapter 10: Setting

Step 3: Determine Attributes

10 Attribute Points (AP) to spend on six PRIMAC attributes:

Score 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
AP Cost -2 0 1 2 3 5 7 10

Or use a Standard Array:

  • Balanced: 5, 4, 4, 3, 3, 2
  • Specialist: 6, 4, 3, 3, 2, 2
  • Focused: 7, 3, 3, 3, 2, 2

Apply species modifiers AFTER spending AP.

Step 4: Allocate Skills

10 Skill Points (AP) to spend on 18 skills:

Rank 0 1 2 3
AP Cost 0 1 2 3
Competence Untrained Novice Apprentice Journeyman

Starting characters cannot exceed Rank 3 in any skill.

Skill Packages (Spend all 10 AP):
  • Explorer: Survival 3, Pilot 2, Tech 2, Perception 2, Athletics 1
  • Specialist: One skill at 3, two at 2, one at 1
  • Soldier: Combat 3, Athletics 2, Discipline 2, Intimidation 2, Survival 1
  • Socialite: Persuasion 3, Deception 2, Performance 2, Commerce 2, Knowledge 1

Step 5: Select Tags

Tag Quick Reference

  • You get 1 tag per skill rank
  • Rank 1 = 1 tag, Rank 2 = 2 tags, Rank 3 = 3 tags
  • Tags provide +1 die when relevant to your action
  • Choose tags that fit your concept and playstyle

See Appendix A for complete tag list, or use skill tree suggestions in Chapter 5

Step 8: Define Motivation

Choose or roll d10:

  1. Survival - Stay alive in a hostile universe
  2. Wealth - Accumulate credits and resources
  3. Power - Gain influence and control
  4. Knowledge - Uncover secrets and learn
  5. Justice - Right wrongs and protect others
  6. Freedom - Break chains and explore
  7. Glory - Become legendary
  8. Love - Protect those you care about
  9. Redemption - Make up for past mistakes
  10. Discovery - Find what's never been found

Step 9: Create Connections

Answer for at least one other character:

  • How do you know them?
  • Why do you trust them?
  • What do you need from them?
  • What can you offer them?

Example: Marcus "Wire" Chen

Following the Nine Steps

1. Concept

Ex-military hacker turned freelance "security consultant"

2. Species

Human (no modifiers)

3. Attributes (10 AP)

  • Presence: 2 (0 AP)
  • Resolve: 3 (1 AP)
  • Intellect: 5 (3 AP)
  • Might: 2 (0 AP)
  • Awareness: 4 (2 AP)
  • Coordination: 4 (2 AP)
  • Deficit: 2 AP (balanced)

4. Skills (10 AP)

  • Technology: 3 (3 AP)
  • Combat: 2 (2 AP)
  • Stealth: 2 (2 AP)
  • Deception: 2 (2 AP)
  • Knowledge: 1 (1 AP)

5. Tags

  • Technology: [Military Tech], [Quick-Hack], [System Exploit]
  • Combat: [Cover Fighter], [Precise]
  • Stealth: [Electronic], [Patient]
  • Deception: [Fast-Talk], [Poker Face]
  • Knowledge: [Military Protocols]

6. Derived Values

  • Health: 12 (10 + MIG 2)
  • Stress: 13 (10 + RES 3)
  • Defense: 14 (10 + COO 4)
  • Mental Defense: 14 (10 + AWA 4)
  • Initiative: 8 (AWA 4 + COO 4)
  • Move: 5 meters

7. Equipment

Working class (400 credits), Military Kit modified for hacking

8. Motivation

Redemption - Make up for military black ops past

9. Connection

"I served with Rodriguez. They saved my life on Kepler Station."

Starting Character Limits

  • Maximum Attribute: 8 (before species modifiers)
  • Maximum Skill Rank: 3
  • Minimum Attribute: 1
  • Total Attribute AP: 10
  • Total Skill AP: 10
