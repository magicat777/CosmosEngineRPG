Chapter 10: Electronic Warfare and Hacking

In a galaxy connected by quantum networks and AI consciousness, information is the ultimate weapon. The Universal Tag System transforms hacking from simple dice rolls into dramatic narratives of digital infiltration, where cyber-warriors battle across virtual landscapes that mirror the complexity of human consciousness itself.

10.1 Digital Battlefields

Electronic warfare encompasses everything from simple system intrusion to full-scale battles between AI consciousnesses. The Tag System creates layers of complexity and narrative opportunity.

Hacking Tags

Approach Tags

  • [Subtle]: Undetected infiltration
  • [Aggressive]: Brute-force attacks
  • [Social]: Human manipulation
  • [Quantum]: Reality-level hacking

System Tags

  • [Secured]: Military-grade encryption
  • [Adaptive]: AI-defended systems
  • [Ancient]: Legacy code vulnerabilities
  • [Sentient]: True AI consciousness

10.2 Cyberspace Navigation

Advanced civilizations visualize data networks as navigable spaces. Hackers don't just type commands—they navigate digital landscapes where data has geography and information has weather.

Virtual Reality Hacking

Full immersion environments where hackers appear as avatars. Physical actions translate to digital effects.

  • Combat becomes digital warfare
  • Physical skills apply in cyberspace
  • Avatar appearance affects interactions

AI Consciousness

True AIs inhabit digital space as their natural environment. Confronting them requires exceptional skill.

  • AIs can reshape virtual environment
  • Home advantage in their own systems
  • Negotiation often more effective than force

10.3 Electronic Warfare Applications

System Infiltration

Breaking into secured systems requires matching your approach to system defenses:

Corporate Systems

Tags: [Commercial], [Layered], [Monitored]

Multiple security layers but predictable patterns.

Military Networks

Tags: [Hardened], [Lethal], [Classified]

Aggressive countermeasures and physical consequences.

AI Core Systems

Tags: [Sentient], [Adaptive], [Mysterious]

Defenses change in real-time based on intrusion attempts.

10.4 Digital Combat Examples

Example: Corporate Data Heist

Scenario: Infiltrating Hyperion Industries' financial records

The Hacker: [Subtle] [Patient] [Corporate-Trained] specialist

The System: [Commercial] [Layered] [AI-Monitored] network

Approach: Using [Corporate-Trained] + [Subtle] tags to mimic legitimate access patterns, the hacker gains +2 dice for appearing to be an authorized user.

Complication: AI security notices unusual access patterns. The [Patient] tag allows the hacker to wait and resume when security attention shifts elsewhere.

Resolution: Perfect tag synergy ([Subtle] + [Patient] + [Corporate-Trained] + [AI-Compatible] gear) grants +4 dice for the final data extraction.
