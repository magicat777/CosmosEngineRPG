Chapter 4: Character Advancement

Characters advance by spending Advancement Points (AP) to increase skills. Each skill rank grants one new tag.

Chapter Contents

  • Core Advancement System
  • Tag Evolution

Core Advancement System

How Advancement Works

  1. Earn AP through play - GM awards based on accomplishments
  2. Spend AP to increase skills - Cost depends on desired rank
  3. Each rank grants one new tag - Choose from skill's tag list
  4. Meet training time requirements - Higher ranks take longer

üìÖ Downtime Training Rules

  • Training time is game time, not real-world time
  • Characters can train during travel or between missions
  • Active adventuring counts as 50% training time
  • Interruption adds 25% to remaining time
  • Can train multiple skills simultaneously at 150% time each

Tag Evolution

How Tags Change with Rank

The same tag grows more powerful at higher ranks. Tags don't change mechanically (+1 die) but their narrative scope expands:

Example: [Precise] Tag Evolution

  • Rank 1-2: "Careful aim" - Take time for accurate shots
  • Rank 3-5: "Perfect timing" - Strike at optimal moments
  • Rank 6-7: "Surgical precision" - Thread impossible gaps
  • Rank 8-10: "Reality-defining accuracy" - Hit across star systems

Tag Synergies

Quick Synergy Rules

  • When multiple tags apply to the same action, they all provide dice
  • Maximum +5 dice from all tag sources combined
  • Players discover synergies through play, not predetermined lists
  • GM has final say on which tags apply

Example: Hacking a Military Database

Character with Technology 4 attempts the hack:

  • [Military Tech] - Familiar with military systems (+1 die)
  • [Patient] - Taking time to avoid detection (+1 die)
  • [Systematic] - Following proper intrusion protocols (+1 die)
  • Total: +3 dice to the roll

Training Options

Self-Directed

Standard Method

  • Full AP cost
  • Full training time
  • Learn through experience
  • No external requirements

Most common advancement method

Instruction

-25% AP Cost

  • Requires teacher 2+ ranks higher
  • Standard training time
  • Access to facilities/materials
  • Regular lessons needed

Good for ranks 1-5

Mentorship

-50% AP Cost

  • Requires master 4+ ranks higher
  • 150% training time
  • Intensive one-on-one training
  • Significant story commitment

Rare but powerful

Special Advancement

Breakthrough Moments

GM may award instant rank increases for:

  • Defeating vastly superior opponent using that skill
  • Achieving "impossible" task under pressure
  • Making galaxy-changing discovery

Attribute Enhancement

  • Cost: 10 √ó new level in AP

Common Questions

Can I save AP?

Yes. Bank AP until you have enough for desired rank.

Multiple teachers?

Use best discount only. Benefits don't stack.

Forgotten skills?

Skills don't degrade. Once learned, always available.

Starting above Rank 1?
