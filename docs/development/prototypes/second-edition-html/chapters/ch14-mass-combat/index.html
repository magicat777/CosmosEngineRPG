Chapter 14: Mass Combat

When individual heroes command fleets and armies, the Universal Tag System scales seamlessly to handle epic conflicts that reshape star systems and topple galactic empires.

14.1 Fleet Combat

Fleet battles use the same core mechanics as personal combat, but with tags representing entire ship groups and strategic doctrines.

Fleet Tags

Composition Tags

  • [Balanced]: Mix of ship types
  • [Heavy]: Capital ship focused
  • [Fast]: Fighter and corvette swarm
  • [Carrier]: Fighter screen tactics

Doctrine Tags

  • [Aggressive]: Close-range assault
  • [Defensive]: Standoff engagement
  • [Tactical]: Coordinated maneuvers
  • [Elite]: Superior training

14.2 Strategic Scale

Mass combat operates on multiple scales simultaneously:

Individual Scale

PC actions within the larger battle

  • Heroic moments
  • Key ship duels
  • Critical objectives

Squadron Scale

Small unit tactics and coordination

  • Wing formations
  • Coordinated strikes
  • Tactical maneuvers

Fleet Scale

Grand strategic movements

  • Battle lines
  • Strategic objectives
  • Victory conditions

14.3 Command and Control

High-ranking PCs influence mass combat through their Command skill and leadership tags:

Admiral Actions

  • Fleet Maneuvers: Reposition entire battle lines
  • Tactical Orders: Grant bonuses to allied units
  • Strategic Insight: Exploit enemy weaknesses
  • Inspire Forces: Boost morale and performance

Squadron Leader Actions

  • Wing Coordination: Synchronize attacks
  • Target Priority: Focus fire effectively
  • Evasive Patterns: Reduce incoming damage
  • Combat Adaptation: Counter enemy tactics

14.4 Example: The Battle of Kepler's Gate

Scenario Setup

Federal Fleet: [Balanced], [Tactical], [Experienced] - 200 ships

Pirate Alliance: [Fast], [Aggressive], [Desperate] - 300 ships

Objective: Control of the Kepler Gate hyperspace junction

PC Admiral's Decisions

Admiral Sarah Chen ([Tactical] [Inspiring] [Federation-Trained]) commands the Federal Fleet.

Round 1: Uses [Tactical] + Fleet [Balanced] to form defensive screen

Round 2: [Inspiring] tag rallies wavering cruiser squadron

Round 3: Perfect synergy: [Tactical] + [Experienced] fleet + [Defensive] formation grants +4 dice for devastating counter-attack

Outcome

Federal tactical superiority defeats pirate numbers. Admiral Chen's reputation gains [Victor of Kepler] tag.
