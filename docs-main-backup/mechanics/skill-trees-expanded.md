## Skill Tree Integration

### Cross-Skill Synergies

Some advanced abilities require investment in multiple skill trees:

**Cyber-Samurai** (Combat + Tech)
- Requires: Combat Expert (Striker) + Tech Proficient (Gadgeteer)
- Benefits: Energy weapon mastery, cyber-reflexes, digital combat

**Void Pirate** (Pilot + Intimidation)
- Requires: Pilot Proficient (Ace) + Intimidation Proficient (Predator)
- Benefits: Terror raids, impossible maneuvers, fear aura in space

**Ghost Operative** (Stealth + Deception)
- Requires: Stealth Expert (Spy) + Deception Expert (Shapeshifter)
- Benefits: Perfect infiltration, undetectable identity, memory erasure

**Battle Medic** (Medical + Combat)
- Requires: Medical Proficient (Combat Medic) + Combat Proficient (Defender)
- Benefits: Heal while fighting, weaponized medicine, combat triage

**Psychic Warrior** (Discipline + Combat)
- Requires: Discipline Expert (Psion) + Combat Expert (Tactician)
- Benefits: Mental combat, precognitive dodging, telepathic coordination

### Prestige Paths

Ultimate specializations requiring legendary status:

**Transcendent Being** (Any 3 Legendary)
- Become more than mortal
- Reshape reality at will
- Exist across dimensions

**Universal Constant** (Any 5 Legendary)
- Become fundamental force
- Cannot be destroyed
- Define aspect of reality

**The Omega** (All Legendary)
- Ultimate being
- Rewrite universal laws
- Create/destroy realities
